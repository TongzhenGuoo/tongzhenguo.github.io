Hi, I'm Tongzhen

Graphic Designer Focused on Concept & Visual Systems

I am a graphic designer based in Toronto, working across branding, editorial, and cultural projects.

My practice is grounded in conceptual thinking and system-based design. I approach each project by identifying its context, defining a clear visual logic, and building a cohesive visual language that supports long-term communication rather than short-term visual impact.

What I Do

Visual Identity Systems

Building structured brand languages through typography, color, and layout systems.

Concept-Driven Design

Transforming abstract ideas into clear and accessible visual communication.

Editorial & Information Design

Organizing complex content into readable and engaging formats.
